what is fishbowl

(outside of being a cool name and a great opposite to Shark Tank) a ‘fishbowl’ conversation is a form of dialog that can be used when discussing topics within large groups. fishBowl is not only suitable for large groups but, it provides an advantage by lowering distinctions between the companies pitching and the startups/audience. It makes the environmental inviting and engaging; something that many small companies would never have an opportunity of receiving.

more on fishbowl

fishbowl is an end to end process and solution. We're with you from pitch to production. So how does it work?

  • We start by working with you define the problem in a clear concise way.
  • Then we help you build a presentation that clearly communicates your challenges.
  • Next we go and curate the right startups, creatives, and businesses that can help sole your problem.
  • We host and produce the fishbowl event.
  • Startups and creatives bid on the challenges you have presented.
  • We help you select and chose the companies you want to work with.
  • We manage the relationship and make sure that the challenges get solved.

It's pretty simple, but if you have more quesitons send us a message below.
